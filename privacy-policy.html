Plain language summary: The Unseen March stores your journal entries, mood logs, and grounding preferences on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data with any third party. No account is required. No data leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

The Unseen March is published by Breaking Ranks Books, founded by Wayne Aince (Ret. Senior Master Sergeant, USAF). We are a veteran-owned independent publisher focused on mental health advocacy for veterans, first responders, and trauma survivors.

Contact: wayneaince@gmail.com

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Your mood logs, journal entries, or grounding session data
  • Your location, device identifiers, or IP address
  • Health or medical information of any kind
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device only:

  • Journal entries — text you write in the Field Journal screens
  • Mood check-in logs — your mood ratings and trigger notes
  • Streak and progress data — your chapter completion and daily streak count
  • App preferences — display settings and notification preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to our servers. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

The Unseen March app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with Facebook, Google Analytics, Firebase, or any other third-party service.

The app displays links to crisis resources including the Veterans Crisis Line (988), the Crisis Text Line, and SAMHSA. Accessing those external services is subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Crisis Resources and Emergency Situations

Important: If you are in crisis, please contact the Veterans Crisis Line by calling or texting 988 and pressing 1, or chat at veteranscrisisline.net. This app is a wellness companion — it is not a substitute for professional mental health care or emergency services.

We do not monitor or read crisis resource interactions made through this app. Those connections go directly to the relevant crisis services.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control at all times. You may delete your data at any point by clearing the app storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
